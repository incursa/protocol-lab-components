# Incursa Contributor Agreement v1

This repository requires a contributor agreement for all non-allowlisted
contract, schema, fixture, scenario, suite, load-profile, documentation, and
governance contributions.

By contributing to ProtocolLab, you confirm that:

- you have the right to submit the contribution;
- you are not knowingly submitting third-party material without permission;
- you grant Incursa the rights needed to use, reproduce, modify, distribute,
  and sublicense the contribution as part of ProtocolLab and related project
  material;
- your signed pull request comment may be recorded for compliance and
  auditability.

To sign this agreement on a pull request, comment exactly:

> I have read the Incursa Contributor Agreement and I hereby assign my
> contribution rights as described.

Pull requests cannot be merged until the contributor agreement requirement is
satisfied or the contributor is explicitly allowlisted by maintainers.
